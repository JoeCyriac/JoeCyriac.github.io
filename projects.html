Risk Quantification Project




From June 2021 - August 2021, I worked as an intern at Best Buy as an Information Security Risk Analyst. I had a chance to work with several leaders in Risk Management. During these meetings, we discussed several threats and potential breaches that are seen throughout the year. After conducting over 15 meetings, we then passed the information to another team that was able to put the risks discussed into a dollar amount and rank them by severity.



History Sniffing Website




In my first Information Security class at the University of St. Thomas, my final project was to create a website that sniffs the user's history and recommends a major based off of what they see. Each color on the page is associated with a major at the University of St. Thomas and would be invisible if the link had already been visited. For example, blue is linked to the science major page. An example of how the website works is as following: If the user visting this site only sees the color blue, they would type this in and press the "Submit" button. This would bring them to another page that says they should look into majoring in science.
