Fun

Welcome! On this page, you will get to know a little more about my interests and what I like to do in my free time.



Movies


I love watching movies in my free time. Some genres that I love are Comedy, Action, Adventure, and Mystery. This is one of many things my family loves to do together. I try to watch a new movie every week!

I have been a fan of Marvel characters since I was little and continue to watch all their corresponding movies to this day. The most recent Marvel movie I have seen is Shang Chi.



Sports


The only sport I actively watch is football. My favorite NFL team is the Minnesota Vikings. I have been to one game at the U.S. Bank Stadium in 2017. Every year I watch them lose, but still continue to support them. I hope they win a Super Bowl at some point during my lifetime!


Throughout middle school and high school, I was on the track and field team. A proud achievement of mine is that I made the varsity team freshman year. I participated in the 100-meter dash, 200-meter dash, and the 4x100 relay (which is a relay of 4 runners that run 100 meters each).



Music



This is a picture I took in 2019 of Post Malone and Swae Lee performing the song Sunflower. I love listening to music whenever I can, especially while I am driving and doing homework. Currently, I do not have a favorite genre of music, but I mostly listen to Rap and Hip-Hop. My top 3 favorite artists at the moment are Post Malone, Travis Scott, and Drake.

I also love going to concerts. I have been to 6 so far and hope to go to many more in the future!
